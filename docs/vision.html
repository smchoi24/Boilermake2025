What is Site Sentinel

"Revolutionizing workplace safety with real-time AI monitoring."

About our Mission

Our mission is to harness AI technology to enhance workplace safety by addressing critical challenges such as PPE compliance and unsafe posture. We aim to proactively detect hazards in real time, empower industries to stay compliant, and create safer, more efficient work environments through innovation and AI-powered solutions.

What it does

Site Sentinel uses Roboflow to detect missing PPE (helmets, vests, etc.) and MediaPipe for real-time pose estimation to monitor unsafe lifting techniques. Leveraging edge AI for low-latency processing, the system provides real-time alerts on-site. It also utilizes a cloud-based analytics dashboard to analyze historical safety data, track compliance, and generate detailed reports for both workers and managers.

How Site Sentinel Can Impact Society

By preventing workplace accidents and ensuring OSHA compliance, Site Sentinel contributes to safer work environments, reducing injuries, downtime, and costs. It empowers industries to proactively address safety concerns, improving overall productivity and worker well-being.
