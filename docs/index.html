Who we are

We are Computer Science majors from Purdue University, ranging from freshmen to juniors, passionate about using technology to make a real impact. Our team specializes in AI, cloud computing, and software development, working together to build an AI-powered Industrial Safety Monitoring System that enhances workplace safety.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a world where workplace safety is proactive, not reactive. By harnessing the power of AI and real-time data, we aim to revolutionize the way industries ensure the well-being of their workers
